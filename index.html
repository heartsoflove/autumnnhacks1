About



Between September 10th and September 12th, Hearts of Love will be hosting a hackathon. A hackathon is a coding competition where participants are given a specific time limit to code a project, which is then judged. It will be a 48-hour event filled with many opportunities for our hackers to learn more and become a part of our community. Some of our workshops include intros to computer programming languages and fun events that include games and movies.


The money earned from this event will be used to help purchase living necessities for disadvantaged people, the homeless, and orphanages.


We are Hearts of Love, a non-profit organization that strives towards helping our community become a better place by promoting kindness, teamwork, responsibility, and proactiveness in order to assist the homeless and orphanages that exist in our world.


We currently support over a hundred members on our community platform and have worked to help people at disadvantage.

FAQ


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a coding event where participants are given a set time to code a project.


Who can attend?

Anyone may attend AutumnnHacks, regardless of age, gender, or race! However, if you are under 18, you will need parental consent.


Where will AutumnnHacks be taking place?

This year, AutumnnHacks will be an online-only event.


When will AutumnnHacks be?

AutumnnHacks will be from September 10th to September 12th, 2022.


What if I'm a beginner?

AutumnnHacks is beginner-friendly, so feel free to join! We will have many workshops and mentors to help guide you with your project!


Will I get swag?

Unfortunately, we will not be able to ship everyone swag this year. However, participants will be given digital credit from our sponsors.
